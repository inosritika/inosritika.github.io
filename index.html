01. About Me

Hey, I'm Ritika 👋

Data Scientist at Oracle (Fusion Data Intelligence), Maths & Computing from IIT Delhi. I build the LLM and retrieval systems behind enterprise AI: query routing, RAG, and the engine that decides what to surface and when.

Lately I'm most drawn to real-time voice AI and agentic systems, the parts where a model has to act, not just answer. The problems I enjoy live between ML and systems engineering: keeping latency low, making evaluation trustworthy, and getting agents to reason with tools instead of guessing. It's also why I keep building side projects, from an on-device voice interviewer to hackathon products, just to see how far an idea holds up.

What drives me is curiosity: I want to know how things actually work, not just get them running. The why and the how matter to me more than the what. I'm looking for a team of like-minded people to dig into hard problems with, and keep learning from.
