Data Science Intern @ Oracle — RL for Supplier Selection

Research Intern @ National University of Singapore

Apr 2022 – Jul 2022 · Singapore · Prof. Ganesh Neelakanta Iyer

A research internship at NUS applying machine learning to real-world geospatial and epidemiological data — combining unsupervised structure discovery with supervised forecasting during the Covid-19 period.

  • Ran an unsupervised, exploratory analysis of geolocational data, using the Elbow Method to choose the optimal number of clusters (best k) before modeling.
